Within the frame of a given theme – Sublime Imperfections – we worked on various smaller assignments. Within this process we could choose an artist, whose work we could use as an inspiration for a new sculpture or installation. I chose Thomas Hirschhorn and used his critique on consumer society and the contemporary convention of throwing things away easily, and made an installation of plastic waste materials, which were once functioning as forks, spoons...
